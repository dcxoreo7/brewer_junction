BREWER TOUR GUIDE

Brewer Junction's historic town square has been ranked #1 small town town squares in the country! Come join our celebration of this event this Tuesday! This is a monumental day in Brewer Junction. The celebration will have free food and beverage for everyone attending.

Be sure to stop by the flower shop and hardware store during this event. We can owe this award to them because of their success.

Must See:

We have taken a poll of every visitors favorite thing to do in Brewer Junction and we are happy to announce that our beautiful parks were the favorite of everyones! So be sure to stop by the city parks for a walk to enjoy outside before winter.
